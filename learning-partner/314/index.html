Kay Peterson

Founding Partner, Learning Partners Group

Featured on September 26, 2013

Kay Peterson is an organization development consultant,a Gestalt Professional Certified Coach, and principal of Learning Partners Group and Harlan Peterson Partners.Her strength-based approach combines experiential learning, polarity management and adult development theory to support leadership development, to increase team cohesion, and to build learning organizations that spur collaboration, innovation and social responsibility.She is a co-creator of the innovative Flexibility Model and Summit Series programming for developing vital, flexible leaders and lifelong learners.Prior to consulting, Kay owned and managed a privately held business that provided sales to manufacturers and distributors,opened new lines of business and created sustainable ventures.

Kay earned a Masterof Science in Positive OrganizationDevelopment and Change and a Masterin Business Administrationfrom Case WesternReserve University.In addition, she holds a Masterin Nursing (Clinical Nurse Specialist in Community Health) from Emory University and a Bachelor of Science from Vanderbilt University.Kay earned a Certificatein ExperientialLearning Theory and Practice,a Certificatein Developing Leadership through Emotional Intelligence for Coaching and SustainableChange, and an Appreciative Inquiry

Certificate in Positive Business and Society Change from Case Western Reserve and has pursued post-graduate training at the Gestalt Institute of Cleveland in Gestalt Training, Gestalt Coaching and the highly regarded Working with Physical Process program. Kay is certified in Polarity Management and is studying at the Mastery Level.She has held leadership positions on health care, civic and private boards.Kay believes that everyone can transform through learning.
